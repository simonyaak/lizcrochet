Our Story

Every thread tells
a story of care

LC&C began with a hook, a ball of yarn, and a belief that handmade things carry something machines can't replicate — warmth, intention, and individuality.

Founded in 2022 in Juba, South Sudan, our mission is to empower women artisans through the production and sale of high-quality, handmade crochet and craft products that reflect the rich cultural heritage of South Sudan.

Mission

To provide high quality, handmade crochet and craft items that bring joy and beauty into the lives of our customers — unique, personalised pieces that celebrate creativity and individuality.

Vision

A world where creativity knows no bounds. We strive to empower women artisans to express themselves through crochet and craft, building a community that supports and uplifts one another.
